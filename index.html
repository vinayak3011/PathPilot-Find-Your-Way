What is PathPilot?

PathPilot is a cutting-edge 3D campus map and navigation system designed to help you explore our campus effortlessly. It provides an interactive and immersive way to find your way around, discover key locations, and access important campus information

Why Use PathPilot for Campus Navigation?

The Motive for This Project is To Provide a Complete Guide & Security For the Campus.

Efficiency

PathPilot makes navigating our campus quicker and more convenient.

Unlimited Devices

You can Use it Anytime Anywhere Just By Visiting The Website"

Customization

Tailor your campus experience by saving favorite locations and routes.

Safety

It offers security features to keep you safe and provide assistance in case of emergencies.
